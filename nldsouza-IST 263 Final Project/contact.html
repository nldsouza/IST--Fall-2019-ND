About Film Junction

Film Junction is a video streaming service website that focuses on bridging the gap between independant filmmakers and content and the entertainment industry. Being that there is no exclusive platform for independant filmmakers to showcase their content film junction serves as a plaform to expand to a wider audience in the indie film industry.
